    Settings

    Breathing speed

    Slow breathing (4–12 breaths/min) helps the nervous system calm down.
    Watch the circles and move the slider until the rhythm feels comfortable.

    Dark theme

    Dark theme lowers screen brightness and is more comfortable in low light.

    High contrast mode

    Improves readability with stronger text and UI contrast.

    Large text

    Increases text size while keeping layout readable on all screens.

    Disable gradients

    Quotes switch without fill effect, and canvas lines become solid color.

    Bionic font

    Applies a bionic-friendly reading font to all site text, including quotes and settings.
